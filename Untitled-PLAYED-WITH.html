    The Natural Botanist Bouquet

    A beautiful wild everlasting wild bouquet in a more neutral palette. Made with Banksia, Safflower, Tansy, cream Globe Amaranth & Pepper Grass. Please note each bouquet is unique and may vary slightly from the image. How to care for dried flowers Dried flowers will last up to three years if properly cared for. Do not place in direct sunlight or anywhere where there is too much humidity or moisture or near a heater. To clean use a hairdryer on low speed or take outside and shake down gently.
